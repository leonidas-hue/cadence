## v1 MVP audio inventory target

For a shippable v1 we need:
- **Variant A:** 8 weeks × ~7 sessions = ~56 sessions. Realistic v1 ship: weeks 1–4 fully scripted (28 sessions), weeks 5–8 as repeating/integration content (8 unique sessions). **Total: ~36 unique scripts.**
- **Variant B:** Same structure. ~36 unique scripts.
- **Variant C:** Same structure + 2 pharma educational. ~38 unique scripts.
- **Variant D:** Same structure. ~36 unique scripts.
- **Shared:** ~12 unique scripts.

**Total v1 audio inventory: ~158 scripts.**

This is more than my "~50 scripts" estimate in the spec — that was based on heavier sharing. Realistic estimate now that variant differences are clearer: ~150–160 scripts for v1.

**Production approach to manage scope:**
- Week 1 of each variant must be unique (locks in the user, sets the frame)
- Weeks 5–8 can lean heavily on shared content with variant-specific framing intros (3–5 min "intro" + shared 5–10 min practice)
- Some content is reused across variants with adjusted intros (e.g., box breathing is shared)

## Voice cost projection

ElevenLabs Pro tier: ~€22/mo, 100k character/mo limit. Average script is ~2,500 characters of speech (excluding pause markers). 100k chars = ~40 scripts/mo.

To produce 160 scripts: **~4 months on Pro tier, or 1 month on Creator tier (€88/mo, 500k chars).**

Recommendation: Start on Pro to test voice quality + iterate on scripts. Switch to Creator for the production push once scripts are locked.

## What this means for the project

Variant A users can now go through a complete 8-week experience. That's enough for:

1. **Closed beta with Variant A users only.** Recruit ~15-20 men with anxiety-driven acquired PE (the most common profile by far). Run them through the full 8 weeks. Collect feedback on tone, pacing, session length, drop-off points, what they wish was different.
2. **Voice generation pilot.** Generate audio for all of Variant A in both Adam and Bella. Listen end-to-end. Catch tonal issues, mispronunciations, pacing problems before producing the other variants.
3. **App scaffold work.** With content locked, Phases 3-7 of the build spec can begin in parallel — Flutter scaffold, daily plan engine, logging UI, payments setup.
