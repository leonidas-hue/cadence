# Shared — Edging Practice (Solo, Paced)

**Type:** On-demand practice mode
**Used by:** Variant A (week 2+), Variant C (week 4+), Variant D (week 3+)
**Duration:** ~17 min audio (for 20-25 min self-directed practice)
**Voice direction:** Same Adam / Bella. Calm. Brief. The audio interjects only at planned cue points; most of the session is silence so the user can stay focused on their own internal state.

This module is the canonical paced edging session. It's structured so the user can launch it any time, do the practice with audio guidance, and end. The audio cues key transitions (climb, hold, stop, breathe) but doesn't run continuously.

---

## Pre-session note (shown in app, not in audio)

> Before starting: 20-25 minutes of privacy. Phone on do-not-disturb. Comfortable, private location. If you don't have time for the full session, choose a shorter practice from the library instead. This session expects continuous attention.

---

## Script

[soft]
This is a paced edging session. About twenty minutes total. I'll give you cues at key transitions, but most of the session is yours.

[PAUSE 3s]

Get comfortable. Privacy. Time.

[PAUSE 5s]

We'll start with three minutes of breath. Then four cycles of climb-and-stop. Then a closing minute.

[PAUSE 3s]

Begin with breath.

[PAUSE 1s]

[soft]
In, four.
[BREATH IN — 4s]
Out, six.
[BREATH OUT — 6s]

[soft]
In.
[BREATH IN — 4s]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 6s]

[soft]
In.
[BREATH IN — 4s]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 6s]

[soft]
Continue this on your own. About two more minutes. Then we begin.

[PAUSE 120s]

[soft]
Begin solo arousal work. Light grip. Varied pace. Climb to a six.

(Silent for ~3 minutes — user climbs.)

[PAUSE 180s]

[soft]
Wherever you are, stop. Hands off. Three slow exhales.

[PAUSE 1s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[PAUSE 3s]

[soft]
Wait until you're back at four. About thirty to forty-five seconds.

[PAUSE 45s]

[soft]
Resume. Climb to six again. Maybe seven this time if you're confident.

(Silent for ~3 minutes.)

[PAUSE 180s]

[soft]
Stop. Three slow exhales.

[PAUSE 1s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[PAUSE 3s]

[soft]
Wait. Drop to four.

[PAUSE 45s]

[soft]
Resume. Third cycle. Same pattern.

(Silent for ~3 minutes.)

[PAUSE 180s]

[soft]
Stop. Breathe.

[PAUSE 1s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[soft]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 8s]

[PAUSE 3s]

[soft]
Wait.

[PAUSE 45s]

[soft]
Final cycle. Climb. Then either finish, or stop without finishing. Both are fine.

(Silent for ~3 minutes.)

[PAUSE 180s]

[soft]
Whether you finished or not, take a minute now. Lie still. Three slow breaths.

[PAUSE 1s]

[soft]
In.
[BREATH IN — 4s]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 6s]

[soft]
In.
[BREATH IN — 4s]
Out.
[BREATH OUT — 6s]

[soft]
In.
[BREATH IN — 4s]
Out, fully.
[BREATH OUT — 6s]

[PAUSE 5s]

[soft]
Done.

## Production notes

- Single file: `practice_edging_solo.mp3`
- Total runtime: approximately 20 minutes
- The 3-minute silent segments are intentional. The user is doing real work during them; voice cues would interrupt.
- The 8-second exhale during stop-cycles is harder than 6-second; it's there because the longer exhale is more effective at dropping arousal back down.
- A 12-min "shorter" version with 3 cycles instead of 4 should be produced as `practice_edging_solo_short.mp3` for users with less time available.
