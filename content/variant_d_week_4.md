# Variant D — Week 4: BRANCHED

**Profile:** Porn-Conditioned / Masturbation-Pattern PE
**Week 4 theme:** *The protocol branches based on routing flag.*

This week has two parallel content streams. The app selects based on the routing flag (`porn_current` or `porn_past`) established at intake.

- **Current path** (`porn_current` users): Porn taper begins. Structured frequency reduction.
- **Past path** (`porn_past` users): Solo-to-partnered transfer begins. The taper module doesn't apply.

Both paths converge at week 6.

**Total sessions:** 14 (7 per path)
**Average duration:** 5–8 minutes
**Voice direction:** Same Adam / Bella across both paths. The current path has the most tonally challenging content of the entire protocol — engaging directly with porn use without moralizing. Read with extra care during voice generation.

**Production note:** Each session day below has TWO scripts — Version A (current path) and Version B (past path). The app stitches the right script for each user. File naming for audio production: append `_current` or `_past` to the audio file name (e.g., `d_4_22_framing_the_taper_current_adam.mp3` and `d_4_22_framing_the_transfer_past_adam.mp3`).

## Version B — Past path

The first transfer session today is sensate focus, applied solo.

[PAUSE 3s]

Sensate focus was developed by Masters and Johnson in the 1960s as a partnered exercise for couples. The principle: take the goal out. Make the experience about pure sensation, with no end-state to reach.

[PAUSE 2s]

The solo version applies the same principle. Today's practice.

[PAUSE 3s]

Set aside fifteen minutes. Privacy.

[PAUSE 2s]

Strip down to whatever feels comfortable. Underwear. Naked. Whatever lets you focus on physical sensation.

[PAUSE 2s]

Sit or lie comfortably. Hands free.

[PAUSE 3s]

For fifteen minutes, your only task is to touch your own body — slowly, with attention — and pay attention to what you feel. The whole body, not the genitals.

[PAUSE 2s]

Forearms. Chest. Stomach. Thighs. Face. Scalp. Hands.

[PAUSE 2s]

Vary the touch — light, firm, slow drag, brief brush. Notice which sensations are pleasant, which neutral, which surprisingly intense, which dull.

[PAUSE 3s]

Three rules.

[PAUSE 2s]

[firmer]
One. No genital touching. Not because genital sensation is bad — because we're training the rest of the body to register pleasure on its own merits. For your profile specifically, the genital-focused conditioning has narrowed the field. We're widening it.

[PAUSE 2s]

[firmer]
Two. No goal of arousal. You're not trying to get aroused. Not trying to not get aroused. Whatever happens with arousal, fine. Just keep paying attention to sensation, not agenda.

[PAUSE 2s]

[firmer]
Three. Stay in the body, not the head. If you find yourself thinking about your shopping list — fine, no big deal — just gently bring attention back to whatever you're touching right now.

[PAUSE 3s]

Some honest expectations.

[PAUSE 2s]

This may feel weird the first time. Touching yourself slowly with no goal feels strange to most adults. The mind tries to make it sexual or productive or efficient.

[PAUSE 2s]

You may find some areas surprisingly responsive — places you didn't know were sensitive. You may find others feel numb, less alive than expected. Both are normal. Both useful information.

[PAUSE 3s]

After fifteen minutes, stop. Take a few breaths. Notice how you feel.

[PAUSE 3s]
