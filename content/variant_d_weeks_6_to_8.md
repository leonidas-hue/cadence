- Week 6 — *Convergence.* Current and past paths meet. Zero-use experiment closes for current users. Partnered application begins seriously.
- Week 7 — *Application.* Skills meet partnered sex more deliberately.

**Average duration:** 4–8 minutes
**Voice direction:** Same Adam / Bella. The voice has been carefully non-moralizing for five weeks. Continue that. Some week 6 content lightly references the current-vs-past distinction; mark it where it does.
