# Mindfulness Add-On — "Daily"

**Type:** Universal optional add-on (all variants)
**Duration:** ~7 min
**Frequency:** Daily, if user opted in
**Voice direction:** Slower, softer than the variant sessions. Almost meditative. This is *not* aimed at sexual function explicitly — it's general nervous system training that has independent evidence (Brotto et al.) for sexual function benefit when stacked with the core protocol.

This add-on is the same script every day in v1. In v2, we add a 7-day rotation. In v3, weeks 2 and beyond unlock different scripts (loving-kindness, body scan variants, breath observation, etc.).

---

## Script

[soft]

Seven minutes today.

[PAUSE 2s]

Sit somewhere you won't be interrupted. Back supported. Hands resting in your lap or on your thighs. Eyes can be open or closed — most people prefer closed.

[PAUSE 4s]

We're not going anywhere. We're not trying to feel a particular way. The whole practice is to notice what's already here.

[PAUSE 4s]

Begin with the breath. Don't change it. Just observe it.

[PAUSE 5s]

The way the air enters. The way your body expands a little. The small pause at the top.

[PAUSE 5s]

The exhale. The way the body softens.

[PAUSE 5s]

The pause at the bottom. Then it begins again.

[PAUSE 6s]

If your mind wanders — and it will — that's not a failure. The noticing that you've wandered is the practice. Just come back to the breath.

[PAUSE 8s]

Now bring attention to the body as a whole. Whatever you can feel.

[PAUSE 4s]

Where there's tension, you don't need to release it. You don't need to fix it. Just see it.

[PAUSE 5s]

Where there's neutrality — large parts of the body usually feel neutral, which is also a feeling — see that too.

[PAUSE 6s]

Where there's pleasantness — warmth, softness, ease — see that.

[PAUSE 6s]

Most of us spend our days only noticing the unpleasant. The headache, the tight shoulders, the back pain. The neutral and the pleasant happen quietly, unnoticed. Today, give them a moment of your attention.

[PAUSE 10s]

Now sound. Just for a minute, listen.

[PAUSE 2s]

Don't label what you're hearing. Don't decide whether you like it or don't like it. Just listen. Sounds in the room. Sounds outside.

[PAUSE 30s]

Come back to the breath.

[PAUSE 4s]

[soft]
And one final thing. Wherever you are in your day, however you arrived at this seven minutes — there's something underneath all of it. Something that's just here. Aware. Steady. Not anxious about anything in particular.

[PAUSE 3s]

That part of you is the part we're training. Not the thinker. Not the planner. The one underneath, watching all of it. That's the one that brings you back into the body during sex. That's the one that doesn't get pulled away by performance anxiety.

[PAUSE 4s]

You've been here for seven minutes. Notice what's different.

[PAUSE 5s]

When you're ready, come back. Open the eyes if they were closed. Take a breath that's slightly deeper than the others.

[PAUSE 3s]

[soft]
And go on with your day.
