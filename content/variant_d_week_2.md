# Variant D — Week 2: Grip Recalibration

**Profile:** Porn-Conditioned / Masturbation-Pattern PE
**Week 2 theme:** *The first variable: grip pressure.*
**Total sessions:** 7
**Average duration:** 5–8 minutes
**Voice direction:** Same Adam / Bella. Continued matter-of-fact, mechanical, non-moralizing. The user has been through observation week. Now we ask for the first deliberate change. Tone is direct, not clinical.

**Content philosophy:** Week 1 was observation only — we asked the user to notice without changing. Week 2 asks for the first deliberate behavioral modification: lighter grip during solo sessions. Grip is the right first variable because (a) it's the most directly correctable, (b) it produces the most immediate sensory shift, and (c) it doesn't require touching the porn use question yet (that's week 4-5). This week is universal — applies equally to current and past porn users.

**A note on tone risk:** Asking for behavioral change is the moment the protocol can lose users who came in skeptical. The framing has to be: this is data, not abstinence; you're recalibrating, not depriving yourself; the goal is partnered sex working better, not making solo work worse.
