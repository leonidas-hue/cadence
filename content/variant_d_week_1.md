# Variant D — Week 1: Recalibrating

**Profile:** Porn-Conditioned / Masturbation-Pattern PE
**Week 1 theme:** *Observation, not change. Yet.*
**Total sessions:** 7 (one per day, with same Day 1 → Day 2 immediate-unlock pattern)
**Average duration:** 6–8 minutes
**Voice direction:** Same Adam / Bella voice. The most carefully calibrated tone in the program. Direct. Non-judgmental. Treats porn use as a nervous-system input — not a moral category. Avoids any language that sounds like NoFap, religious framing, or pathologizing. The user did normal things. Their nervous system did what nervous systems do. We're recalibrating.

**Content philosophy:** This profile is the trickiest because the wrong tone alienates the user immediately. The wrong tones to avoid:

1. **NoFap-coded language** — "rebooting," "monk mode," "no PMO," any framing that treats abstinence as morally superior
2. **Religious or shame-based framing** — "addiction," "purity," anything that implies the user has a moral problem
3. **Catastrophizing** — claims that porn rewires the brain in dramatic, irreversible ways. The science is more modest than the popular narrative.
4. **Gendered or political framing** — leaving any of that out

The right tone: matter-of-fact, mechanical, lightly humorous when it lands, focused on *outcomes* (better partner sex) not *purity* (no porn).

**Critical structural choice:** Week 1 is observation-only. We do *not* ask the user to stop using porn yet. We ask them to notice their patterns. The full taper protocol starts in week 2, after the user has bought into the framework. Asking for cessation in week 1 = high dropout.

**Branching for past vs current users:** Variant D routes both currently-heavy users and past-heavy users with residual grip/pace patterns. The app branches certain Day 1 and Day 5 paragraphs based on the routing flag (`porn_current` vs `porn_past`). Branched sections are clearly marked with `🔀 BRANCHED CONTENT` headers. The conceptual content is identical for both populations — only the framing of current vs past behavior differs. Week 4 also branches at the program level (current users get the taper module; past users get extended solo-to-partnered transfer work).

# Session 3 — "The grip"

**Duration:** ~7 min
**Type:** Lesson + light experiment

[PAUSE 2s]

[PAUSE 2s]

When real sex delivers less pressure, the nervous system has two failure modes — either it can't get there at all (delayed ejaculation, paradoxically), or — more commonly in your profile — the contrast is so jarring that any sufficient stimulation pushes it over the edge fast.
