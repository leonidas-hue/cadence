# Variant B — Weeks 5–8: Integration

**Theme arc:**
- Week 5 — *Halfway recheck.* PT visit if not yet done. Refinement of the arousal-focused work. Life integration of release skills.
- Week 6 — *Bringing release into life.* Generalizing release work beyond the practice context.
- Week 7 — *Partnered application.* Or extended solo if not partnered. The release approach during real sex.
- Week 8 — *Maintenance and the long view.* Hypertonic patterns can return; lifelong maintenance is real.

**Total sessions:** 28 (7 per week)
**Average duration:** 4–8 minutes
**Voice direction:** Same Adam / Bella. Slow and unhurried. Continued gentle tone.

**Content philosophy:** The second half of Variant B follows the same lighter-content pattern as the other variants but with an additional emphasis: hypertonic patterns are particularly prone to recurrence under stress. The maintenance work in week 8 reinforces this directly — this is lifelong work, not eight-week work.
